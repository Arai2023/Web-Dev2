Travel to Italy

Have you ever visited Italy?

Italy is a country in Southern and Western Europe. Located in the middle of the Mediterranean Sea, it consists of a peninsula delimited by the Alps and surrounded by several islands. Italy shares land borders with France, Switzerland, Austria, Slovenia and the enclaved microstates of Vatican City and San Marino. It has a territorial exclave in Switzerland and an archipelago in the African Plate . Italy covers an area of 301,340 km2 with a population of nearly 60 million; it is the tenth-largest country by land area in the European continent and the third-most populous member state of the European Union. Its capital and largest city is Rome.

Fun facts about Italy

  • Italy is one of Western Europe's youngest countries
  • Rome is over 2,000 years old
  • Italy's last king ruled for just 36 days
