Recipe #1

  • Time:45 min- 1 hour
  • Serves:Chicken fillet in oven
Ingredients Quantity
Chicken fillet 1
Tomato 4
Cheese 1
Collezione 1

Step 1: Take a chicken fillet and make small incisions.

Step 2: Add spices: salt, black pepper, paprika, garlic.

Step 3: Add the tomato slices.

Step 4: Place in the preheated oven for 40 minutes.

Aaand that's it. You can make garnier if you want to.
